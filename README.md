# LevUp: Systematic Alpha Engine (Indian Equities)

### **Project Overview**
This repository contains a fully systematic, regime-aware, long-only equity strategy developed for the Indian Equity Market. The pipeline replaces traditional mean-variance optimization with a **Multi-Objective Genetic Algorithm** constrained by **Hierarchical Risk Parity (HRP)** to target geometric growth while minimizing tail-risk.

**Key Performance (2010–2020):**
* **CAGR:** 24.41% (vs. Nifty 500: 9.35%)
* **Max Drawdown:** -16.98% (vs. Nifty 500: -38.3%)
* **Information Ratio:** 1.08

## 🛠 My Key Contributions

As the Quantitative Developer responsible for the **Alpha Generation and Risk Management** modules, I designed and implemented the following:

### 1. Market Regime Detection Engine
I developed a macro-filter to shift the portfolio between **NORMAL** and **DEFENSIVE** modes based on market breadth.
* **The Logic:** Calculated the percentage of stocks trading above their $SMA_{200}$.
* **Signal Smoothing:** Implemented a dual-moving-average crossover ($SMA_{30}$ vs $SMA_{150}$) on the breadth data.
* **Hysteresis Band:** Applied a $\pm1\sigma$ volatility band to the crossover to prevent "whipsawing" (frequent, false entries/exits) during sideways markets.

### 2. Cross-Sectional Quadrant Engine
To identify leadership within the universe, I built a 2D classification system that moves beyond simple sector-based picking.
* **Dynamic Sorting:** Stocks are mapped into four quadrants based on **Market Capitalization** and **Smart-Beta (Inverse Volatility)**.
* **Regime-Specific Alpha:** * In **Normal Markets**, the engine prioritizes 3-month Price Inertia and Relative Strength.
    * In **Defensive Markets**, the engine automatically pivots to the **Gain-to-Pain (GtP) Ratio**, prioritizing stocks that exhibit the lowest downside deviation.

### 3. Adaptive Exit Protocols
I moved away from "static" stop-losses, which often fail in high-volatility regimes, and implemented an adaptive system:
* **Volatility-Adjusted Stops:** Developed a trailing stop-loss based on a $k \times ATR_{14}$ (Average True Range) multiplier, customized by sector volatility.
* **Multi-Factor Exit Triggers:** Integrated a "confirmation" logic where a non-emergency exit requires a simultaneous failure in both **Trend (EMA flip)** and **Volume (Negative VPT/OBV Divergence)**.

## 📈 Methodology & Math

The strategy utilizes a complex optimization layer to manage the signals generated by my engine:

* **Scoring Function:** $$Score = \frac{R_{relative} - \text{MAD}(R)}{\sigma_{downside}}$$
* **Risk Constraint:** The Genetic Algorithm (GA) is penalized if the portfolio concentration in any single HRP cluster exceeds 35%, ensuring structural diversification even when momentum signals are strong.
